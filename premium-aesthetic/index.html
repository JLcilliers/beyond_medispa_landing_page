Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

Our medical professionals conduct comprehensive consultations to assess your skin, discuss your goals, and recommend the most suitable treatments. We take time to understand your lifestyle and expectations, ensuring every recommendation aligns with your desired outcomes and comfort level.

Are the treatments painful?

We prioritize your comfort throughout every procedure. Most treatments involve minimal discomfort, and we use topical numbing creams when appropriate. Our medical professionals are skilled in gentle injection techniques, ensuring your experience is as comfortable as possible while maintaining treatment efficacy.

How long do results typically last?

Treatment longevity varies based on the specific procedure and individual factors. Anti-wrinkle treatments typically last 3-4 months, dermal fillers 12-18 months, and skin treatments like HydraFacial provide immediate results with cumulative benefits through regular sessions.

What makes Beyond Medispa different from other clinics?

Our exclusive Harvey Nichols partnership provides unmatched luxury and privacy. All treatments are performed by qualified medical professionals, never beauty therapists. We combine medical expertise with personalized service in prestigious locations, ensuring exceptional results and experiences.

Is there downtime after treatments?

Most of our treatments require minimal to no downtime. You may experience slight redness or swelling immediately after certain procedures, but this typically resolves within hours. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and are available for any post-treatment concerns.
